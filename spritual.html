Pilgrim's Progress

India is its billion people. And it is safe to say that these people define what spirituality in India is. The most prominent religions in the country are Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Buddhism, Sikhism and Jainism. Apart from these there are several other schools of thoughts. The country is filled with many spiritual destinations which should not be missed by anyone looking for the true Indian experience. These are open and welcome to everyone.

Nestled in Pamban Island that is shaped like a conch, Rameswaram, in Tamil Nadu, is a quaint town that is connected to the Indian mainland by the 2-km-long Pamban bridge.
Snuggled between the Arabian Sea and the UNESCO World Heritage Site of the Western Ghats, Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of the beautiful state of Kerala, almost touches the southern tip of India.
One of the oldest living cities, Madurai, holds the soul of Tamil Nadu in its magnificent and grand temples that are among the finest and most awe-inspiring specimens of architecture in the country.
